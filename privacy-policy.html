05 How Information Is Used

  • To respond to messages sent through the contact form.
  • To detect and block spam, bot traffic, and abusive form submissions.
  • To understand which pages and projects visitors find useful, and improve site performance and content accordingly.
  • To remember your theme and performance preferences between visits.
  • To maintain the security and stability of the site.

Data is never sold. It is not used for targeted third-party advertising, and it is not shared with data brokers.

08 Data Sharing & Disclosure

Information is shared only with the service providers listed in Section 7, strictly to operate this site, and may be disclosed if required by law, court order, or to protect the security and legal rights of Syed Ahmer Shah or site visitors. Beyond that, no personal data collected here is shared with any other third party.

09 Data Retention

Contact form messages are retained only as long as needed to respond to and resolve the inquiry, and are deleted periodically thereafter unless there's an ongoing reason to keep them (e.g. an active collaboration). Aggregated analytics data is retained per the retention windows of Vercel Analytics and Google Tag Manager, typically limited to a rolling window of a few months to a couple of years.

10 Your Rights & Choices

Regardless of where you're visiting from, you can email support@ahmershah.dev at any time to:

  • Ask what data (if any) is held about you from a contact form submission.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your contact form submission and associated data.
  • Ask a question about this policy or how your data is handled.

Requests are typically handled within a reasonable timeframe, generally within 30 days.

11 Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children and is not knowingly used to collect personal information from anyone under the age of 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the contact form, please email support@ahmershah.dev and it will be removed.

12 Security

This site is served exclusively over HTTPS, enforces a strict Content-Security-Policy, and uses reCAPTCHA plus a honeypot field to reduce spam and abuse on the contact form. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so while reasonable measures are taken, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

13 External Links

This site and its connected profiles link out to third-party platforms (GitHub, LinkedIn, Medium, Coursera, YouTube, and others listed in Section 3). Once you leave ahmershah.dev, that platform's own privacy policy and terms apply — this policy does not extend to third-party sites, even ones Syed Ahmer Shah maintains an official presence on.

14 International Visitors

This site is operated from Pakistan and is accessible globally. By using it, you understand that your information may be processed on servers located outside your own country, subject to this policy and the policies of the third-party providers listed above.
